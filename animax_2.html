1. Admissions to any course shall be purely based on the discretion of the members of Academic Council,ANIMAX INSTITUTE OF MULTIMEDIA.

2. During the training period, the student shall strictly comply with all the rules of discipline in
force.

3. Students shall maintain good conduct within the premises, Director,ANIMAX INSTITUTE
OF MULTIMEDIAmay terminate the training of any student in following circumstances,

a. 75% of the attendance not maintained.

b. Fee not paid in stipulated time.

c. Causing hindrance to the smooth flow of classes in the laboratory.

4. Fee once paid shall not be refunded under any circumstances. However refund of the course
fee shall be made if the course could not be conducted at all due to the reasons beyond the
control of the management of ANIMAX INSTITUTE OF MULTIMEDIA. In such cases the
refund shall be made after deducting the administrative expenses along with deduction of the
fee for the software covered.

5. ANIMAX INSTITUTE OF MULTIMEDIA shall make genuine efforts to assist the student for
placement. No assurance/guarantee is given in this regard.

6. Student shall pay any tax or charges levied by the Govt. of India, during the course of study,
if applicable.

7. In case of payment by monthly installments, the student shall remit the installment amount within 10th day of each month, failing which ANIMAX INSTITUTE OF MULTIMEDIA may charge penalty. Student shall be terminated if the installments are not paid in time. However the officer in charge of the center may allow the student to attend till the end of the month on genuine reasons and if requested in written with attached evidence.

8. Student shall get valid receipt immediately after making any payment.

9. All payment shall be made only in front office / to the authorised persons & receipt should be collected immediately. No payment shall be made to the teaching staff.

10. Payment receipts shall be preserved by the student till the certificate is received. Certificate request without valid payment receipts shall not be entertained.
